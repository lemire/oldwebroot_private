MATH 2023

Daniel Lemire, Ph.D.

Course Info (Winter 2002)

Vector functions and their derivatives. Line integrals, Infinite series. Differential equations: first and second-order linear equations and application; series solutions.

TextBooks

  • McCallum, Hughes-Hallett, Gleason: Multivariable Calculus. Wiley. ISBN 0-471-31151-0
  • Bronson: Schaum's outline of Differential Equations. 1994. ISBM 0-07-008019-4.

COURSE OUTLINE

Vector functions and their derivatives (5 weeks)

Reference : McCallum, Hughes-Hallett, Gleason: Multivariable Calculus. Wiley.

  • Line integrals;
  • Grad, Curl;
  • Green's theorem, Stoke's theorem;

Series (4 weeks)

Reference : Mathematics 2023 - Infinite Series I (in-house publication)

  • Convergence;
  • Bounded series;
  • Taylor series;
  • Radius of convergence.

Differential Equations (6 weeks)

Reference : Schaum's outline of Differential Equations.

  • Linear Differential Equations;
  • Second-Order Linear Differential Equations;
  • The Method of Undetermined Coefficients;
  • Variation of Parameters;
  • Initial-Value Problems;
  • Series solutions;
  • Eigenfunction Expansions.

Maple might be used for this course.

There are two marking schemes. The second one is meant to help the students who would do poorly on the Mid-Term and the assignments.

Assignments will be made of a large set of problems and students will have roughly two weeks to complete each set. Team work will be allowed but each student must write his own paper. These problems will be similar to what you can expect during the exams so students are encouraged to work the problems on their own whenever possible.
