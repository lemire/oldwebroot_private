 Review by Shara M. Hamshar

"This book certainly will peak your interests. I literally could not put it down! There is a distinct writing style to each author that made for interesting reading. Some stories were tasteless while others really intrigued me. As a woman reading this book, I found I liked the stories written by other women the most. The experiences they described (in immense detail might I add) were very realistic and are sure to get your mind going!

Those with short attention spans like myself will find the short stories that make up this large book easy to conquer. If you like it, then The Mammoth Book of International Erotica will have to be your next night time reading project. I hope you enjoy it as much as I did!"
