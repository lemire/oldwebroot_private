Une famille d'ondelettes biorthogonales sur l'intervalle obtenue par un schéma d'interpolation itérative

Abstract

In earlier work, we showed that we could get Cohen-Daubechies-Feauveau biorthogonal wavelets by deriving a certain number of times the fundamental functions of the Lagrange iterative iterpolation. We designed a new iterative interpolation scheme from which we get wavelets on the interval with filters having only rational numbers. As a particular case, we get Donoho's average interpolation. (Full paper in French with extended abstract in English.)

Keywords

Subdivision schemes, compactly supported wavelets, wavelets on the interval, fundamental functions.

Reference
